Our Story

ABOUT CTS

CTS
Our Mission

CONNECTING BUSINESSES TO THE WORLD

Founded in 2003, CTS Cargo & Transport Solutions began as a regional freight carrier in the Middle East. Over two decades, we've grown into a global logistics powerhouse serving clients across 150+ countries.

Our mission is simple: deliver every shipment with the same care and precision we would give our own — on time, every time, with full transparency from pickup to delivery.

Today, CTS operates a fleet of 800+ vehicles, manages 2.5 million sq ft of warehouse space globally, and employs over 4,500 logistics professionals worldwide.

Our Vision

THE FUTURE OF LOGISTICS

We believe logistics is the backbone of global commerce. As trade evolves, so do we — investing in AI-driven route optimization, IoT tracking, and sustainable transport solutions to lead the industry forward.

CTS is committed to reducing our carbon footprint by 40% by 2030 through electric fleet expansion and green warehousing initiatives.

Our vision: a world where every business, regardless of size or location, has access to world-class logistics infrastructure.

Service 06

COLD CHAIN LOGISTICS

Unbroken temperature-controlled logistics for pharmaceutical, food, and sensitive cargo — maintaining product integrity from origin to destination.

Overview

INTEGRITY MAINTAINED AT EVERY TEMPERATURE

Temperature-sensitive products demand a logistics partner with specialized expertise, certified infrastructure, and zero tolerance for temperature excursions. CTS Cold Chain Logistics maintains the full cold chain from pick-up to delivery with continuous monitoring and full traceability documentation.

Our GDP-compliant cold chain network serves pharmaceutical companies, food manufacturers, grocery retailers, and biotech firms — handling products ranging from fresh produce to deep-frozen biologics.

  • Ambient, chilled (+2°C to +8°C), and frozen (-18°C to -25°C) capabilities
  • Ultra-low temperature (-60°C to -80°C) for cryogenic and biotech cargo
  • GDP-certified warehousing and transport for pharmaceutical products
  • IoT temperature monitoring with real-time alerts and data logging
  • Refrigerated vehicles with dual-temperature compartments
  • Passive packaging solutions (dry ice, gel packs, PCM) for air freight
  • Full traceability and batch documentation for regulatory compliance

TEMPERATURE RANGES

  • Ambient: +15°C to +25°C
  • Chilled: +2°C to +8°C
  • Frozen: -18°C to -25°C
  • Deep Frozen: -40°C
  • Cryogenic: -80°C

CERTIFICATIONS

  • GDP (Good Distribution Practice)
  • ISO 9001:2015
  • HACCP Certified
  • IATA CEIV Pharma
  • Halal Certified Storage
